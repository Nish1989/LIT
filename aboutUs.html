About Us

We are a NGO established in the year 2011 by the alumni of PSG Institute of Management, Coimbatore. It was founded on the principle that if one person is educated he can achieve anything in this world. It is with this belief in mind that Learn India Trust was launched with a vision to make sure that everyone can afford a quality education, healthy life and peaceful environment to live. And to make it possible LIT has chosen three different paths to take our common vision forward, LIT has chosen three different paths to take our common vision forward, Vizhuthugal , Sakthi and Uyir.
