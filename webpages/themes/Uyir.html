Uyir

"UYIR" is a green group of LIT. A campaign started by planting seed samples. It aims to build a green revolution. By planting trees, we make up for the loss we have caused the planet and ourselves through the destruction of forest areas over the centuries. Recognizing the importance of tree planting as both a practical means to conserving the environment and as an effective awareness raiding activity, LIT's UYIR is engaged in and spear-heads a number of tree-planting activities around Tamil Nadu. We also spread awareness about the importance of planting trees among young children and the youths of the nation.

Upcoming Projects

1. To plant 1000 trees in and around Tamil Nadu
2. To conduct Go Green campaign and awareness programs in Schools and Colleges.
