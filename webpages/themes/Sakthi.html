Sakthi

"SAKTHI" works to improve the health status of the rural and urban communities by conducting periodic medical camps. We also promote holistic health and well-being by advocating, encouraging and guiding positive 'health-seeking behavior' among the people. SAKTHI is also planning to provide free meal for the needy and food items to Orphanages.
