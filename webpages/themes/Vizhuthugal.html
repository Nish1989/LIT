Vizhuthugal

"VIZHUTHUGAL" is working towards providing quality education for the under-privileged children. Our aim is not just funding their education, we also try to bring out their talent, dream and skill, and make a clear path for every kid to achieve their goal. We also offer educational materials, school supplies and technology. We sponsor children to attend high schools and help to build learning centers to promote education to children in poverty.

Recent Projects

1. Team LIT has adopted 2 kids Vijayan and Karthick from Inam Manichi, near Kovilpatti (Tuticorin Dist) Tamil Nadu. We take care of all their Educational Expenses and basic necessities.
2. Becoming an Engineer is Mr. Karthick’s Dream. And LIT has made him to fulfill his dream. Now Karthick is Studying BE in Chennai.
